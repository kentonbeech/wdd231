About East London

East London, South Africa, was established in 1847 as a British settlement and became an important port for trade and military operations during the colonial era.

Sales of the C-Class Mercedes-Benz

As of 2024, the Mercedes-Benz's East London plant remains the sole manufacturer of the C-Class sedan in South Africa, contributing significantly to local sales. The Mercedes-Benz plant in East London has produced over 50,000 units of the C-Class sedan annually. In 2023, local sales of the C-Class accounted for approximately 30% of total Mercedes-Benz sales in South Africa.

Stellenbosch University

Stellenbosch University, established in 1918, is a prestigious institution located in the scenic Western Cape of South Africa. Renowned for its commitment to academic excellence and innovation, it offers a diverse array of programs across various fields. One of its standout features is the Stellenbosch University Choir, celebrated as one of the best university choirs in the world. The choir has gained international recognition for its exceptional talent and performances, showcasing a rich tapestry of musical traditions. With a vibrant campus culture and a strong emphasis on research and community engagement, Stellenbosch University not only cultivates academic leaders but also nurtures a deep appreciation for the arts and cultural heritage.
