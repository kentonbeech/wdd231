Site Name

Stirling Tennis Club. I chose this name as I live in Stirling.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to foster a vibrant community of tennis enthusiasts, by providing information on club events, membership opportunities, and local weather info. Visitors can access schedules for practicess and tournaments, sign up for lessons, and stay updated on local news.

Scenarios

  • Are there any local tennis clubs near me?
  • How can I sign up to a tennis club?
